Document Management System

SGEDI is a document management platform that gives public and private companies a wide range of tools and aids for easy management of all your documents.

SGEDI Allows companies to control the creation, storage, revision and distribution of documents, increasing the efficiency in the ability to reuse information; As well as controlling the flow of documents. It integrates everything essential for document management, collaboration between users and advanced search functionality, in one easy-to-use solution. The application includes administrative tools to define the roles of the different users, quotas for each user, security at the document level, a complete log of activity and the configuration of automatic tasks.
